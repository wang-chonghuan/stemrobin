## Design note — quiz-drawer kept generic (G-B)

`quiz-drawer.tsx` is content-agnostic (injected `fetchQuestions`/`record`, optional
`attempts` API). Its `!attempts` branch also serves logged-out **lesson** learners
(`if (!attempts || !isIn)`), so it is not dead. Per charter §2/§3 the optional path
was left intact; only misleading story comments were removed. (See `im-grill.md`.)
